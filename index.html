This project investigates strip-loaded waveguide designs on the lithium niobate on insulator platform, with an emphasis on optical confinement, fabrication compatibility, and device-level integration for photonic applications.

Project 02 Prototype

ScAlN d31 Characterization

This work focuses on the experimental characterization of the d31 piezoelectric coefficient in scandium aluminum nitride thin films, aiming to support reliable material evaluation and performance benchmarking for electromechanical devices.

ScAlN d31 Measurement Piezoelectric Films
Project 03 Experiment

Acousto Electric Amplifier on LNOI Platform

This project explores acousto-electric amplification mechanisms on the LNOI platform, with the goal of understanding wave-matter interactions and enabling new functionalities for signal control and integrated acoustic devices.

Acousto-Electric LNOI Platform Signal Amplification

This study examines periodically poled lithium niobate structures for acoustic nonlinearity, targeting improved control of nonlinear acoustic behavior and broader opportunities for wave manipulation in advanced material platforms.
