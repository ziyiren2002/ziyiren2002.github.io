A Campaign Concept for 21GRAMS

Never Again

Never Again is a campaign concept developed for 21GRAMS, built around one warning: diseases that feel historical can return the moment we stop trusting the vaccines that pushed them into the past.

We Want To Tell Young parents and young adults who think vaccine-preventable diseases belong to the past.
That The only reason these diseases look like history is that vaccines made them so.
Because The moment we stop believing in vaccines, the past can return.
