Civic Awareness Campaign

Your Vote, Your Voice

This concept uses missing-voice language and poster-led visuals to frame voting as an issue of presence, agency, and representation, pushing the audience to see political silence as something urgent rather than abstract.

Direction A copy-led awareness campaign that turns absence, silence, and missing participation into the central visual idea.
Format Poster executions and supporting campaign visuals that balance direct civic messaging with bold graphic framing.
Tone Urgent, public-facing, and persuasive, with a visual language that treats every missing vote as a missing voice.
