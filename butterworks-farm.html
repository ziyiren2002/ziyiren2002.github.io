Brand Visual Concept

Butterworks Farm

This project builds a softer, farm-rooted visual world around Butterworks Farm, using warm product imagery and rural cues to create a brand feeling that is handcrafted, grounded, and inviting.

Direction A brand concept centered on warmth, simplicity, and the tactile feeling of farm-made goods.
Visual Focus Clean product storytelling with rustic references that make the brand feel small-batch, human, and rooted in place.
Tone Gentle, wholesome, and grounded, with a visual rhythm that emphasizes craft over spectacle.
