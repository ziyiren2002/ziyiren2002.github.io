Advertising Concept

RAID

This RAID concept treats household pest control like a dramatic product intervention, using cinematic scale, tension, and sharp visual contrast to make the brand feel immediate, powerful, and unmistakable.

Direction A high-impact art direction that turns ordinary kitchen spaces into stylized stages for product power and confrontation.
Visual Hook Oversized product presence, swarming insects, and theatrical perspective shifts that make the can feel dominant and decisive.
Tone Bold, dramatic, and slightly cinematic, with a polished household backdrop that keeps the concept grounded in everyday use.
