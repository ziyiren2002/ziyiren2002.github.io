A Playful Social-First Campaign

Whiskas

This Whiskas concept imagines cats as energetic lifestyle characters, turning everyday attitudes, habits, and food moments into humorous social content built for quick attention and brand recall.

Direction A light, witty campaign voice built around confident cat personalities and social-first visual hooks.
Format Hero visuals, short-copy posters, and Instagram-story-style content designed for fast, playful consumption.
Tone Playful, mischievous, and affectionate, with the product world folded naturally into each cat scenario.
